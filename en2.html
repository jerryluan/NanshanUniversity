Administrative Regulations for Foreign Students

I General rules

Article 1: Combining the specific situation of our school, these provisions are formulated for the purpose of strengthening administrative affairs for foreign students of our university, according to the Administrative regulations for universities accepting foreign students issued by Ministry of Education of People¡¯s Republic of China.

Article 2: The foreign students referred in this regulation are students with valid foreign passport, who register in our university to receive degree education and non-degree education.

¢ò Types of Foreign Students

Articles 3: According to types of study, foreign students are divided into degree studying students (junior college students, undergraduate) and non-degree studying students (short-term Chinese language studying students, long-term Chinese language studying students, normal advanced students and senior advanced students).

¢ó Requirements and Admission of Foreign Students

Article 4£ºJunior college students, undergraduate: academic record equivalent to Chinese Senior High School Graduate, to be in good health, 18-60 years old, HSK grade 4. For students whose Chinese language is not proficient enough may enroll first in an one-year Intensive Chinese Language Study Program. When they obtain Certificate of HSK Grade 4, they will transfer to study their specialties.

Article 5: Non-degree student: 18-60 years old and to be in good health.

Article 6: Those applicants who meet all the above requirements fill out YANTAI NANSHAN UNIVERSITY APPLICATION FORM FOR INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS and provide copies of all the required documents. Send directly all the required documents to International Exchange & Cooperation Office at Yantai Nanshan University (YNU). In at most 10 days after receiving the application and relative materials, the International Exchange & Cooperation Office will send the admitted applicant a YNU Admission Notice, a Physical Examination Report for Foreigner, and a Visa Application Form (JW202 for self-supporting students).

¢ô Registration

Article 7: During the registration time specified on the YNU Admission Notice, the admitted student must bring the YNU Admission Notice, the Physical Examination Report for Foreigner and JW202, register at International Exchange & Cooperation Office at YNU, pay for tuition fee, learning materials fee and other fees, provide 4 photos of 1-inch and 8 photos of 2-inches.Those who drop out of school because of his or her own reasons should note that the tuition fee is non-refundable after it is paid. Students who do not register during the specified period should inform International Exchange & Cooperation Office in advance.

Article 8: After registration, degree student and non-degree student with notice of International Exchange & Cooperation Office come to relevant schools or colleges to register and select courses.

Article 9: International students study at our university with student visa should purchase personal accident insurance provided by People¡¯s Life Insurance Company of China.

Article 10: During the period of registration, International Exchange & Cooperation Office transact the process of valid residence for international students who hand in all the required materials at required time, but visa fee should be paid by the international students themselves. International students who do not hand in all the required materials at required time shall go to Entry-Exit Inspection and Quarantine Department of Yantai Police Bureau in person to transact residence permission procedure.

Article 11: International Exchange & Cooperation Office transacts student ID for freshman. International students, who have studied at our university for one or more semesters, register at International Exchange & Cooperation Office within one week at the beginning of each semester. Without approval, those international students, who do not return school to register at the required time, will be viewed as absenteeism, and those whose absence time is more than one moth, will be viewed as dropping out of school automatically.

¢õStudy and Discipline

Article 12: foreign students should abide by study disciplines, go to class on time, unexcused absence, being late, leave early are not allowed.

Article 13: Degree students¡¯ course study, evaluation, asking for leave and terminating leave should be in accordance with relevant students¡¯ enrollment status management regulation of the university.

Article 14: International students enjoy China¡¯s festival and the university¡¯s summer and winter vacation. The university does not give holiday to international students of their native festival and holiday.

Article 15: The main task for international students is study. In the premise of do not influencing study, the government of China permits international students to engage in study-work activities relevant to one¡¯s university majors.

¢ö Graduation, Completion and Non-completion of Schooling

Article 16: Degree students who complete all the courses provided by the training program and complete the total credits will be permitted to graduate and given diploma. Undergraduate who go through the thesis will be awarded relative bachelor¡¯s degree.

Article 17: Non-degree students who complete all the courses provided by the training program and get qualified scores will be given certificate.

Article 18: Foreign students who leave school (transfer, suspension, drop out from school, certificate, and graduate) should go to International Exchange & Cooperation Office to get Leaving School Notice and transact the process of leaving school as stipulated in the notice. Students should go to relevant department with leaving school notice to settle accounts, return borrowed items, books, and certificate, get the notice stamped and so on. According to the stamped notice, the university transacts the process of leaving school for international students.
